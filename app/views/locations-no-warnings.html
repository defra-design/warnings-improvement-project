Locations not in flood warning areas

You can increase the area around your location to improve your chances of it being covered by a flood warning. You can also link your location to a nearby flood warning area.

How to increase the areas and link locations?

You can increase the area:

  • individually for each location by clicking the location name
  • for one or more locations at the same time by selecting the locations you want to change and entering the area in the box below
You can also link a location to a nearby flood warning area by clicking the location name.
