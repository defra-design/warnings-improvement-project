Customise flood alerts and warnings

You can choose whether to receive severe flood warnings, flood warnings or flood alerts for each location. This can be more effective for your organisation and help you get a manageable number of warnings and alerts.

You can change to the default option of severe flood warnings and flood warnings for all locations

What are severe flood warnings, flood warnings and flood alert?

Severe flood warnings
When a severe flood warning is issued there is a danger to life and a risk of major disruption.

Flood warnings
When a flood warning is issued immediate action is needed. There is a risk of flooding to homes, businesses, rail and roads.

Flood alerts
Flood alerts warn that flooding may be possible. They are usually issued from two days to two hours before flooding might happen. Alerts can help you be prepared to act on your flood plan.
