How to upload a spreadsheet with X and Y coordinates

  • Download and fill in the X and Y coordinates template (xls)
  • you will need X and Y coordinates in separate fields
  • all coordinates must be 6 figures, without the letter prefix. This gives precise location to a one metre square. For example, coordinates for Nelson’s Column are:
    • X coordinate: 530000
    • Y coordinate: 180400
    • you do not need to include the grid reference (TQ300804)
  • the 'criticality' field allows you to prioritise your locations based on which are most important to your business. If there is a flood, you can filter by criticality and act on the most important locations first
  • if you are not using our template, you can match your fields to ours after you upload your file. We will ask for:
    • location name
    • X coordinate
    • Y coordinate
    • location reference (optional)
    • location type (optional)
    • location team (optional)
    • action to be taken (optional)
    • criticality (optional)
    • notes (optional)
  • you can upload your spreadsheet on the next page
