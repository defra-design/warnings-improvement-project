Get flood warnings by text, phone or email

Use this service to sign up for warnings for flooding from:

  • rivers (fluvial flooding)
  • the sea (coastal flooding)
  • groundwater (when water underground that is usually held in the rocks and soil gets so high that it flows above the surface)

This service does not give warnings for:

  • surface water flooding (flash flooding, a sudden local flood, typically due to heavy rain)

Local authorities are responsible for giving information about surface water flooding. It varies by local area, happens quickly and is difficult to predict. Find your local council (opens in new tab) for more information.

You can use this service to get warnings for yourself or for more than one place on behalf of the organisation you work for.

Start

Before you start

You’ll need to provide:

  • the location or locations you want flood warnings for
  • an email address
  • a way to contact you at any time of day or night - you can choose to get a phone call, text or email

Update your details or flood warnings

If you have previously signed up for flood warnings, you can sign into your account to:

  • update your details
  • choose to no longer receive warnings for a property
  • choose to receive warnings for additional properties
  • delete your account

If you live in Scotland, Wales or Northern Ireland
