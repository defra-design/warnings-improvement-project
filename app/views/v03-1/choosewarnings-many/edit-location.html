What are flood warning areas and flood alert areas?

Flood warning area
Flood warning areas are geographical areas where flooding is expected, for which you can get flood warnings and severe flood warnings. They usually cover areas where people live or work, such as cities, towns, villages or hamlets. Not all locations are covered by a flood warning area.

Flood alert areas
Flood alert areas are geographical areas where flooding may be possible, for which you can get flood alerts. A flood alert area may match a flood warning area or may cross a number of flood warning areas. Flood alerts are issued earlier than flood warnings to provide advance notice of the possibility of flooding. They may be issued when there is less confidence that flooding will happen.
